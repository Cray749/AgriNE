AI Executive Summary

The Assam Valley region demonstrates a stable overall soil health index (72/100), but reveals localized acute Phosphorus deficiencies in the northern tea-growing estates. Historical yield data indicates a 4% decline in output correlating directly with these deficient zones. The climate resilience score remains robust due to consistent monsoon patterns, though early-warning indicators suggest shifting precipitation distributions requiring adaptive irrigation strategies.
